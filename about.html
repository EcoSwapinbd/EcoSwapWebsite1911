About EcoSwap

Our Mission

EcoSwap is a community-driven environmental initiative focused on reducing plastic pollution and promoting sustainable living. Our mission is to encourage people to recycle plastic waste by exchanging plastic bottles and bags for eco-friendly rewards such as trees and reusable bags. By creating a simple exchange system, EcoSwap motivates individuals, students, and communities to actively participate in protecting the environment and building a greener future.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a cleaner and greener society where plastic waste is properly managed and reused. Through awareness campaigns, community programs, and plastic collection initiatives, EcoSwap aims to reduce environmental pollution and inspire positive environmental action.

Our Team

EcoSwap is powered by a passionate team of volunteers who believe in protecting the environment. Our team organizes plastic collection programs, community events, and awareness campaigns to inspire people to reduce plastic waste and support environmental sustainability. Together, we are working towards a cleaner and healthier planet for future generations.
