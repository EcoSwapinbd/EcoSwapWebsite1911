About EcoSwap

Turning Plastic Waste into a Greener Planet

Our Mission

EcoSwap is dedicated to reducing plastic waste and promoting environmental sustainability. Our mission is to encourage people to collect and donate plastic bottles and bags so that we can transform them into trees and eco-friendly products. Together, we can make our planet greener and cleaner for future generations.

Our Vision

We envision a world where communities actively participate in plastic recycling and sustainability initiatives. By creating reward-based programs, we motivate people to contribute positively to the environment while building awareness about the harmful effects of plastic pollution.

Our Values

  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Community Engagement
  • Education and Awareness
  • Transparency and Trust
  • Innovation in Sustainability
