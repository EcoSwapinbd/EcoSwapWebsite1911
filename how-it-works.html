How EcoSwap Works

Turning Plastic Waste into Environmental Impact

EcoSwap is a community-driven environmental initiative designed to reduce plastic pollution. Our mission is to encourage people to recycle plastic bottles and bags by exchanging them for eco-friendly rewards. Through this program, individuals, students, and communities can actively participate in protecting the environment. By donating plastic waste, participants help reduce pollution, support recycling efforts, and contribute to building a greener planet.

4 Simple Steps

1. Collect Plastic

Collect plastic bottles or plastic bags from your home, school, or community.

2. Register Online

Register on our website and inform us about the plastic you want to donate.

3. Drop-off or Pickup

Bring your plastic waste to our collection point or request pickup if available.

4. Get Your Reward

Receive your reward such as trees or eco-friendly reusable bags.
