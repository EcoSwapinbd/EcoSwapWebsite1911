How EcoSwap Works

Follow these simple steps to help reduce plastic pollution

1. Collect Plastic

Collect plastic bottles and plastic bags from your home, school, or community.

2. Register Online

Fill out the EcoSwap online form to register your plastic donation.

3. Drop-off / Pickup

Bring the plastic waste to our collection point or request a pickup service.

4. Get Reward

Receive eco-friendly rewards such as tree planting or reusable bags.
