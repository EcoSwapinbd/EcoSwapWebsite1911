EcoSwap FAQ

EcoSwap is an environmental initiative where people exchange plastic waste for eco-friendly rewards like trees or reusable bags.

You collect plastic bottles or plastic bags, register online, and bring them to our collection point. After verification you receive your reward.

10 plastic bottles → 1 tree 10 plastic bags → 1 eco-friendly reusable bag.

You can visit our collection point shown on the Map page or contact us for pickup.

Go to the Join / Donate page, fill the form, and start contributing to the environment.
