Our Programs

EcoSwap Environmental Programs

EcoSwap works to reduce plastic pollution and promote environmental sustainability. Through our programs, individuals can donate plastic waste and receive eco-friendly rewards while contributing to a cleaner planet.

Plastic Bottle Program

Collect and donate plastic bottles to help reduce pollution. For every 10 plastic bottles donated, EcoSwap plants one tree to support environmental restoration.

Donate Bottles
Plastic Bag Program

Plastic bags are harmful to nature and wildlife. Donate 10 plastic bags and receive an eco-friendly reusable bag as a reward.
