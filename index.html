Our Mission & Vision

EcoSwap is a community-driven environmental initiative to reduce plastic pollution and promote sustainability. We encourage individuals to donate plastic bottles and bags in exchange for eco-friendly rewards like trees and reusable bags. Our vision is a cleaner and greener society, where communities actively participate in protecting the environment.
